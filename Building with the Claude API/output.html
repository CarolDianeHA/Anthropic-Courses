# One-Day Marathon Race Day Preparation Meal Plan

Here is a one-day meal plan for an athlete preparing for a marathon race with carbohydrate loading for optimal endurance performance:

* **Calorie Target:** Approximately 3,200 calories
* **Macronutrient Breakdown:** Protein (120g), Fat (80g), Carbs (480g)

## Meal Plan:

* **Breakfast (7:00 AM):** Oatmeal (100g dry weight) with honey (30g), banana (150g), and raisins (40g). Low-fat milk (240ml).
  * Protein: 15g, Fat: 8g, Carbs: 110g

* **Mid-Morning Snack (10:00 AM):** Rice cakes (60g) with jam (40g) and a glass of orange juice (250ml).
  * Protein: 4g, Fat: 2g, Carbs: 80g

* **Lunch (1:00 PM):** Grilled chicken breast (130g) with white rice (150g cooked) and steamed carrots (150g). Olive oil dressing (15ml).
  * Protein: 38g, Fat: 12g, Carbs: 95g

* **Afternoon Snack (3:30 PM):** Sports energy drink (500ml) with rice crackers (50g) and dried fruit mix (60g - raisins, cranberries, banana chips).
  * Protein: 5g, Fat: 3g, Carbs: 105g

* **Dinner (6:30 PM):** Baked lean beef (120g) with sweet potato (200g) and steamed green beans (150g). Light olive oil (10ml).
  * Protein: 35g, Fat: 15g, Carbs: 60g

* **Evening Snack (8:30 PM):** Bagel (100g) with honey (20g) and banana (120g).
  * Protein: 8g, Fat: 2g, Carbs: 75g

* **Pre-Sleep Hydration (30 minutes before bed):** Sports drink (250ml).
  * Protein: 0g, Fat: 0g, Carbs: 15g

---

**Key Features:**
- **Nut-free formula:** All nuts and tree nuts completely eliminated due to allergy
- **High carbohydrate loading:** 480g carbs support maximum glycogen storage for marathon endurance
- **Adequate protein:** 120g supports muscle recovery and maintenance
- **Moderate fat:** 80g provides energy without digestive heaviness before race
- **Hydration emphasis:** Multiple fluid intakes throughout the day
- **Easy-to-digest foods:** Simple carbs and lean proteins minimize GI distress during the race

This meal plan prioritizes carbohydrate-rich whole grains, fruits, and lean proteins while maintaining safe hydration, all without any nuts or tree nuts.

# One-Day Marathon Competition Day Meal Plan

Here is a one-day meal plan for an athlete preparing for marathon competition with carbohydrate loading and high caloric intake for endurance performance:

* **Calorie Target:** Approximately 3,500 calories
* **Macronutrient Breakdown:** Protein (130g), Fat (90g), Carbs (520g)

## Meal Plan:

* **Breakfast (6:30 AM):** Oatmeal (120g dry weight) with honey (40g), banana (150g), and whole milk (240g). Toast with jam (2 slices whole wheat bread, 60g total with 30g jam).
    * Protein: 18g, Fat: 12g, Carbs: 130g

* **Pre-Workout Snack (8:00 AM):** Sports drink (500ml) with energy bar (65g).
    * Protein: 10g, Fat: 8g, Carbs: 75g

* **Mid-Morning (10:00 AM):** Bagel (90g) with cream cheese (30g) and sliced turkey (60g). Orange juice (250ml).
    * Protein: 20g, Fat: 12g, Carbs: 85g

* **Lunch (1:00 PM):** Pasta with marinara sauce (200g cooked pasta, 150g sauce) and ground turkey (100g). Side salad with olive oil dressing (150g greens, 15ml oil).
    * Protein: 35g, Fat: 18g, Carbs: 95g

* **Afternoon Snack (3:30 PM):** Rice cakes (50g) with peanut butter (25g) and honey (20g). Apple (150g).
    * Protein: 10g, Fat: 12g, Carbs: 75g

* **Early Dinner (6:00 PM):** White rice (200g cooked) with grilled chicken breast (120g) and steamed carrots (150g). Dinner roll (40g) with butter (10g).
    * Protein: 32g, Fat: 15g, Carbs: 95g

* **Evening Snack (8:00 PM):** Banana (120g) with low-fat yogurt (200g) and granola (40g).
    * Protein: 15g, Fat: 13g, Carbs: 65g

This meal plan prioritizes easily digestible carbohydrates and moderate protein to maximize glycogen storage while maintaining energy levels for optimal marathon performance.
